Artificial Intelligence (AI) is used for mostly analysing imagery data such as X-ray and MRI scans. It has only started developing in biomedical science for the past four years, but has been growing in other fields for more than sixty years. AI examines the images for irregularity by comparing them with average human anatomy scans. This process makes it easier for doctors to distinguish which concern could be in a patient. AI can also organise and store patient data in efficient filing systems. AI is still fairly new, however, so the capabilities are still being discovered.
